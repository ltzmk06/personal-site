About Me

Welcome to my personal website. I'm Manoj, a dedicated software developer with a passion for crafting elegant and functional Software Products.

My coding journey began in the 8th grade with the aspiration to create games reminiscent of Subway Surfers, Temple Run, and Hill Climb Racing. However, back then, my programming skills were far from reaching that level of complexity.

To bridge the gap between my ambition and abilities, I decided to specialize in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) during 11th grade. While my peers were immersed in competitive exam preparations, I delved into Java and C++, reserving my focus for board exams.

My philosophy revolves around the belief that 'we reap what we sow.' I prioritize honing my skills over mere rote memorization. After all, the ability to apply knowledge effectively surpasses the art of blindly reproducing facts in exam papers.
